Privacy Policy

Popcorn Master · Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the game Popcorn Master ("the Game", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and your choices. By playing the Game and signing in, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The Game works without an account. You can play all game modes without providing any personal information.

If you choose to sign in with Google (to save and compare high scores), we receive the following from your Google account:

  • Your display name (only your first name is shown publicly).
  • Your profile picture.
  • A unique account identifier (used to save your best score).

We also store the high scores you achieve, along with the game mode and date.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To let you sign in.
  • To save your personal best score.
  • To display a public leaderboard showing players' first names, profile pictures and scores.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell your personal data.

3. Public Leaderboard

If you sign in and submit a score, your first name, profile picture and score may be visible to other players on the in-game leaderboard. If you do not want this, do not sign in.

4. Third-Party Services

The Game uses Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore) to handle sign-in and store scores. Your data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your score record is kept so it can appear on the leaderboard. You can ask us to delete your account data and scores at any time by emailing us (see Contact). We will remove your data within a reasonable time after verifying the request.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We rely on Google Firebase's security infrastructure to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
